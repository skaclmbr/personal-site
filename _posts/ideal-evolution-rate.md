There is a zen parable about going in circles - the cycle of despair of thinking in 2D, and ignoring the third dimension.

Technology is like that. A new tech comes out, and you fall into one of two camps - it's useful to you so you invest time and energy into making the most out of the technology. 2 years later, the middle folks have stopped using it, move on to other tech, so in order to stay relevant, the builder creates new functions, or changes the
way it works to enable new innovation (best case), or just to put a new spin on the useful (or obsolete) technology. This requires all of us to constantly innovate to keep up.

What keeps us involved? No one is driving the bus - except society.


What is the ideal pace of evolution in any space?
